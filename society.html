Join Our Community

Why You Should Join Us

All members have the right to participate in our various activities, and enjoy the following benefits:

Benefits

  • 20% discount on specialized workshops and courses.
  • Access to exclusive publications.
  • 10% discount on products from our catalog.

Membership Categories

  • Active Member: Members who pay the annual fee.
  • Associate: Those who are nominated for the next assembly and automatically become active members.

How to Become a Member

To join us, you need to fulfill the following requirements:

Requirements

  • Submit a membership application with your general information.
  • Provide a letter of motivation supported by a current member.
  • Pay the required fee.
  • IMPORTANT: Your provisional admission will be approved by the Board and subject to the next assembly for final approval.

Membership Fees

Contribute to our activities with the following fees:

  • Fee: $200 annual fee for professionals and general public, $100 annual fee for students (includes a welcome gift).
